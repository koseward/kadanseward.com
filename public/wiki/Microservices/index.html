Microservices

Overview

Microservices are a popular Software Architecture, often used in modern, large-scale web services, such as Amazon or Netflix.[That one source] As described by Red Hat, a major open source developer, Microservices involve breaking down a larger software endeavor into smaller pieces, which are then developed individually in smaller developmental teams and are individually releasable.[red hat] Each of these services are fine-grained, and have a low level of coupling.[context.understanding of microservices] Often these sections are separate, and are not accessible to teams, giving each piece of the system a privacy that other architectures lack.

History

Microservices are a type of the Service Oriented Architecture. The difference being that while SOA involve all of the systems of a company, microservices involve only the pieces of an individual system.
